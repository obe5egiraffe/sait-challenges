Open House

Oct. 18 & 19, 2019
9am – 3pm

  Add to your calendar

Your future starts here.

Join us at Open House on Oct. 18 and 19, 9 am - 3 pm to explore our programs, discuss your options and connect with industry-trained instructors and fellow students. 

Whether you're just beginning to explore your options, have already decided what you'll study or want to take your career to the next level, our faculty and staff will inform and inspire you by the time you leave campus.

We're looking forward to seeing you at Open House.  



 

Get the most of your Open House experience

Information fair booths

Go straight to the source for information and learn more about our programs, career planning services, student support, how to pay for your education, housing and transfer options.

View booths »

Try-a-skill

Get a feel for your future through an interactive activity — try a new state-of-the-art simulator, create something new or test yourself for talents you haven't even considered yet.

Learn more »

Take a tour

Explore SAIT's unique learning spaces, labs, classrooms and tools that help students get hands-on experience to be career-ready or watch a demonstration on the coolest equipment on campus.

Apply for free

Your first online application is free! Oct. 18 and 19 we'll waive the application fee. Speak with representatives from the Office of the Registrar to get your application and admissions questions answered.

Youth offerings

Learn more about our youth offerings, including SAIT Summer Camps, dual-credit and children and youth programs.

Fuel up

Stop by Starbucks or Tim Hortons for a coffee and snack. Hot and cold lunch options will be available to purchase as well.

Are you an international student?

Join us at Open House to speak with a student advisor from our International Centre and learn how you can become a student at SAIT. Get information on how to apply, obtain study permits/visas, pathways to permanent residency and more. Family members and friends who want to collect information on behalf of someone in another country are also welcome to attend Open House.

Transit and parking

During event hours on Saturday, Oct. 19, free parking will be available in lots P1 and P6 (level one only), with accessible parking available in P6. Regular rates apply to all other lots on campus. 

Our main campus is in the inner city, so the CTrain stops right on campus (SAIT/ACAD/Jubilee Station) with many bus routes providing direct service to SAIT.

 

Shapers. Makers. Originals.

We're built on a passion to shape new worlds as entrepreneurs, leaders and community builders. 

A SAIT education offers students practical experience and theoretical knowledge in a real-world context. You'll benefit from hands-on learning in unique labs and classrooms, participation in applied research with industry partners and — in many programs — workplace practicums to strengthen your resumé upon graduation.

Get connected

We'd love to hear from you — use #SAIT and follow @SAIT on Facebook, Twitter and  Instagram.
